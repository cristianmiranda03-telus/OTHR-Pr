# EMA Bounce with RSI Confirmation Gold Scalper

**Asset:** XAU/USD · **Timeframe:** M5

- **50 EMA** for trend and pullback level; slope (e.g. over 3 bars) confirms direction.
- **RSI 14**: long when RSI 40–70, short when RSI 30–60.
- **Pullback:** price touches or comes close to the 50 EMA (configurable points).
- **Candle:** strong bullish (long) or bearish (short) candle at/near EMA; entry after its close.
- **SL:** 5–10 pips beyond the confirmation candle (below low / above high).
- **TP:** 1:1.5 risk-reward from SL distance.

Attach to XAU/USD M5 chart. Adjust `InpPipsPerPoint` for your broker (often 10 for gold).
