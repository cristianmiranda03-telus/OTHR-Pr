# Nyxian Velocity Scalper

**XAUUSD M1, NY 8:00–11:00 AM EST.**

SMC-style zones (simplified as swing high/low) + **Velocity Vortex** (GVO volume spike ≥ 2 SD above SMA(20) + IIO RSI(3) extreme reversal) + **Rejection Shadow** (long lower wick, close well above wick low; next candle closes above wick low). Entry at open of candle after confirmation. SL 1.5×ATR beyond rejection wick, TP 2.5 R. Optional breakeven at 1R and ATR trailing.

- **GVO:** sum of tick volume over 5 bars; GMVS when ≥ SMA + 2×SD.
- **IIO:** RSI(3); long when was < 10 then crosses above 20; short when was > 90 then crosses below 80.
